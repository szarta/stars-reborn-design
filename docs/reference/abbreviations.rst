**Race design econ notation example:**

.. code-block:: text

   1000   12/9/17-g   11/3/16

Decoded as:

- ``1000`` — 1 resource per 1000 colonists
- ``12/9/17-g`` — 10 factories produce 12 resources/year; factories cost 9
  resources to build; 10k colonists operate 17 factories; ``-g`` = factories
  cost 1 kT less germanium to build
- ``11/3/16`` — 10 mines produce up to 11 kT minerals/year; mines cost 3
  resources to build; 10k colonists operate 16 mines

     - In My Humble/Honest Opinion

     - Off Topic
